About CLB Productions

CLB Productions is a video game development company that specializes in allowing minors to have full access to the ZEBEDEE platform and create games under it, and to redesign existing/popular games and turn them to a play-to-earn game. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to express themselves through the medium of video games and strive to make that a reality for young creators whether they have the resources of AAA companies, or not. One thing we require though from you is a hosting service. (Heroku, DigitalOcean, etc.)

About 2048 Bitcoin

2048 Bitcoin is a unique and challenging game that is based on the popular game 2048, but instead of the main goal being to get the highest score, the main goal is for players to earn sats. The goal of the game is to earn as many sats as possible by combining tiles.
